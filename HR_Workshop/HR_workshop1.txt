How to prepare for interview:
-----------------------------
understandig the market of the company:
1. purpose
2. clients
3. competitors
4. global/local company

who is the interviewer:
1. HR
2. Technical
3. manager

Understanding the job:
1. whats critical for success in the job?

Understanding the DNA of the company and cultural compaibility


Purpose of the interview:
-------------------------
1. Can i do the job?
2. Do i want to do the job? (money is ok to want, but shouldnt be noted as a driving factor) (body language, excitement, passion)
3. Can i integrate easily? (questions like: what is a bad trait of yours? usually questions are based and past experience)


Body language:
--------------
practice power posing. power posing body language will raise self-confidence.


First Impression:
-----------------
%55 body language, expressions and appearance
%38 tone and tonality of speech
%7 speech content


What to do before interview:
----------------------------
1. dress respectfully. jeans not recommended (unless its dark and smooth)
2. arrive 20 mins before interview.
3. soak up the organizational "atmosphere".
4. go over your own CV.
5. fill-in questionnare given to me.
6. good odor (deodorant).
7. shave
8. backpack (and put all things in backpack, not on table).
9. Dont arrive too early (puts interviewer in some "stress").
10. ask who is the interviewer (HR, technical, and name of interviewer/s)
11. i am not responsible for "breaking the ice". i need to "flow"


Start of interview:
-------------------
1. brave handshake (dont initiate handshake, let them initiate)
2. eye contact when handshaking
3. 80% eye contact during interview
3. smile
4. sit up straight


hatzaga atzmit:
---------------
1. Name
2. Academic education
3. relevant qualifications
4. organizational experience
5. strong points/characteristics/qualifications
6. mikud batchum
7. express motivation
